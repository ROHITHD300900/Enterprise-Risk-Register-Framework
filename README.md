# Enterprise Risk Register Framework

**A comprehensive framework for enterprise-wide IT and cyber risk identification, assessment, and management.**

## Overview

This repository provides a **complete, reusable risk register framework** designed for organizations of any size to:

- 🔍 Systematically identify IT, cyber, compliance, and operational risks
- 📋 Maintain a living, tracked risk register
- 📊 Score risks using a standardized likelihood/impact model
- 🔗 Link risks to existing and required controls
- 👥 Assign risk ownership and treatment plans
- 📊 Visualize risk landscape with heat maps and trend reports

## Risk Scoring Methodology

### Likelihood Scale (1-5)
| Score | Definition | Frequency |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Rare | < 1% annual |
| 2 | Unlikely | 1-10% |
| 3 | Possible | 10-30% |
| 4 | Likely | 30-70% |
| 5 | Almost Certain | > 70% |

### Impact Scale (1-5)
| Score | Financial | Operational | Reputation |
|-------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | < $10K | < 1 hour | Minor |
| 2 | $10K-$100K | 1-4 hours | Negative feedback |
| 3 | $100K-$1M | 4-24 hours | Customer loss |
| 4 | $1M-$10M | 1-3 days | Material damage |
| 5 | > $10M | > 3 days | Brand damage |
